Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join Houston Discord?
Simply click the "Join Now" button and you'll be redirected to our Houston Discord server. If you don't have a Discord account yet, you'll be prompted to create one. It's free and takes less than a minute.
Is there a cost to join?
Nope! Houston Discord is completely free. We're a community-driven server with no membership fees or paywalls.
Do I need to live in Houston to join?
While most of our members live in the Houston area, we welcome anyone with a connection to Houstonâ€”whether you're planning to move here, used to live here, or just love the city.
Are there age requirements?
Yes, Houston Discord is an 18+ server. All members must be 18 years or older to join and participate.
What kind of channels do you have?
We have many channels covering everything from food and gaming to politics and pets. Popular channels include #general, #food-drinks, #bookclub, #gaming, #sports, and location-based channels for different Houston neighborhoods.
How do I participate in events like Friendsgiving or Secret Santa?
Event announcements are posted in our events channels. Simply keep an eye out for sign-up posts, or ask in #general about upcoming events. Our major events fill up quickly, so join early!
Can I promote my business or events?
We have specific channels for classifieds and event planning. Please review our server rules and post in the appropriate channels. Self-promotion outside designated areas may result in removal.
How active is the server?
Very active! We average hundreds of messages daily, with peak activity during work hours (we won't judge your procrastination). There's almost always someone online to chat with.
